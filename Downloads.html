Now we're treating you with an actual English translation of the entire novelization of Devil May Cry 4, which fills in a lot of backstory and explanations to things from the game.
The translation isn't entirely perfect, but we've made it as comprehensive and accurate as possible. You might encounter a grammatical error or two but it's not going to make it at all confusing.
