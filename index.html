About The Original Devil May Cry

The Original Devil May Cry is a non profit fan project from a small team of two people passionate about the franchise and is dedicated to the original franchise and everything about it.

It consists of Facebook pages, a large group and a fansite full of DMC goodness that you wouldn't run out of, and it continues to get regularly updated with new content.
