2001

Devil May Cry

Devil May Cry, the first video game in the legendary Devil May Cry franchise. The game was originally intended to be Resident Evil 4 in 1999, but eventually ended up as an entirely different game and was released as Devil May Cry for the PlayStation 2 in 2001.

2003

Devil May Cry 2

Devil May Cry 2 is the sequel to Devil May Cry. It is the first Devil May Cry game that has no involvement by the series creator, Hideki Kamiya. It was directed by Hideaki Itsuno and written by Katsuya Akitomo, Masashi Takimoto and Shusaku Matsukawa.

2005

Devil May Cry 3

Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening is the third game in the Devil May Cry franchise, which serves as a prequel to the first game featuring a young Dante. It was directed by Hideaki Itsuno and written by Bingo Morihashi and Takayasu Yanagihara.

2006

Devil May Cry 3 Special Edition

Devil May Cry 3: Special Edition is an updated version of Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening that features new content, such as a more balanced difficulty as well as the ability to play as Vergil and more. It was released in 2006.

2007

Devil May Cry Animated Series

Devil May Cry: The Animated Series is a 12 episode series based on the Devil May Cry franchise, and takes place between Devil May Cry 1 and Devil May Cry 4, serving as an interquel. It was written by Toshiki Inoue and directed by Shin Itagaki.

2008

Devil May Cry 4

Devil May Cry 4 is the fourth game in the Devil May Cry franchise. It serves as an interquel that takes place between Devil May Cry 1 and 2, and features a new protagonist called Nero which shares the spotlight with the returning Dante. It's directed by Hideaki Itsuno and written by Bingo Morihashi.

2012

Devil May Cry HD Collection

A remastered collection that includes Devil May Cry, Devil May Cry 2 and Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening Special Edition. It was released for the first time on April 4th, 2012 for the Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3, and was re-released on March 13th, 2018 for the PC, Xbox One and PlayStation 4 platforms.

2015

Devil May Cry 4 Special Edition

Devil May Cry 4: Special Edition is a remastered version of Devil May Cry 4, featuring a large amount of new content such as the ability to play as Vergil, Lady and Trish and more. It was released in 2015, nearly 7 years after the original.

2019

Devil May Cry V

Devil May Cry 5 is the fifth game in the Devil May Cry franchise. The game will features the return of Dante and Nero, as well as introducing a new protagonist called V, and is said to take place sometime after Devil May Cry 4. It's directed by Hideaki Itsuno and is expected to be released in March 2019.
