Troubleshoot & FAQ

 How can i Install APK on My Device?

* Download The APK File on Your device.
* Go To Phone Settings > Security and there Enable " Unknown Sources".
* Install The APK File You Downloaded.

  One of the Books got a Error "Unfortunately -App Name- has been stop working" What i do?

Device running Android Lollipop Or Android emulators on PCs May Face This Error, This Error Can be fix by Closing and Re-Opening The App.

How can i apply Devil MAy Cry PhotoBox On my Phone? 

Here are the steps to apply the Devil May Cry PhotoBox to your phone.
* Tap your screen and hold for a few seconds on your home screen until it pop-up, then select Wallpapers.
* Swipe to left ← & choose Devil May Cry PhotoBox. If you don’t see it there, click on Live Wallpapers and choose Devil May Cry PhotoBox from there.  Click Here To View Image Guide
* Samsung users can apply the Devil May Cry PhotoBox by going into Samsung Themes > Wallpapers > My Wallpapers > View All and then tap on the three-dot menu there and select Live Wallpapers. 

 I Install Devil May Cry Novels and Font is Too Small

Open the novel then Tap on screen and go to More > Preferences > Font Size.
