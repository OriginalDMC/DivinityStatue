 How can i Install APK on My Device

* Download The APK File on Your device.
* Go To Phone Settings > Security and there Enable " Unknown Sources".
* Install The APK File You Downloaded.

 How can i apply Devil May Cry PhotoBox or V-Power App to my Phone

Here are the steps to apply the Devil May Cry PhotoBox or V-Power to your phone.
* Tap your screen and hold for a few seconds on your home screen until it pop-up, then select Wallpapers.
* Swipe to left ← & choose Devil May Cry PhotoBox or V-Power. If you don’t see it there, click on Live Wallpapers and choose Devil May Cry PhotoBox/V-power from there.
